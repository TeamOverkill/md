Potentials¶

static double energy(Atoms &atoms)¶

Calculate the energy using a Lennard-Jones potential which is given by

\[ U_{ij}^{LJ} = 4 \pi \epsilon \left( \left( \frac{\sigma}{r_{ij}} \right)^{12} - \left( \frac{\sigma}{r_{ij}} \right)^6\right) \]
